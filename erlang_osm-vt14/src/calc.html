Splits A and B into Split number of similar length lists and spawns the same number of processes that will add each corresponding sublist of A and B in parts.

Each subprocess will message the ParentPid process with {N, Result}, where N is its sublist number and Result is the sum of the addition including carry bits, and the tuple {carry, Carry} where Carry is the highest order carry out of the addition.
